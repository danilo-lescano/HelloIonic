Battle Assistant

This is a Battle Assistant for DnD encounters!

You can access the menu to create a catalogue of monster and team parties to generate fast battles.


- Parties: here you can create the party of your players or a fierceful wolf pack for the next encounter.


- Monster: here you can create the patterns for some creatures or NPCs.

Example: a beholder has 11d8 + 44 hp, or you also can set an static value for this particular monster. Besides that the creature has a bonus of +6 on its initiative (improved initiative and dexterity) that can be rolled automatic when placed on the battle, that likewise can be set as static if you wish.
